The Auction House

What is the Auction House?

The Forza Horizon 4 is exactly what it sounds like. The auction house is were you do bid on cars to get the at a lower price. For example, in the car showcase a Mclaren Senna costs 1,000,000 CR (credits). I you look in the auction house,it will be around 200,000 CR to 300,000 CR. If you place a bid on a car then you will be able to see it on the "My Bids" page. You can also auction your cars by the "Start Auction" To check your auction's, go on the " My Auctions " page.
